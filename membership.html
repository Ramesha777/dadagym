Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes. All DaDaGym plans are no-contract. You can cancel from your member dashboard and keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is the payment on this site real?

Not yet — checkout is currently a simulation for demo purposes. It will be replaced with a real provider (Stripe) in a future release.

Do you offer family or student discounts?

Reach out via the contact form and our team will be happy to help with custom or group pricing.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade any time from your dashboard — the change applies on your next billing cycle.
